Nikki Bhati Case (2025)

A high-profile case from Greater Noida that drew national attention and sparked discussions about dowry harassment, domestic violence, and women's safety.

The case involved the death of Nikki Bhati after she suffered severe burn injuries at her residence. Following complaints filed by family members, police launched a major investigation and arrested multiple accused persons.

Investigators examined witness statements, medical records, digital evidence, and forensic findings as part of the inquiry.
